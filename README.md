## About Me

I'm a Senior DevOps and Platform Engineer based in the United States, focused on the space where cloud infrastructure, automation, and developer experience meet. I like taking messy, manual, fragile systems and turning them into platforms that teams actually enjoy using — self-service, observable, and hard to break.

Most of my work lives around Kubernetes, CI/CD, GitOps, and infrastructure as code. I also wear an SRE hat when reliability matters, and I've done a fair amount of enterprise integration work as a MuleSoft platform architect. Currently I'm engineering platform and delivery tooling at CPKC.

---

## What I Do

- **Platform Engineering** — Build internal platforms and paved paths so product teams can ship without fighting infrastructure.
- **Kubernetes & Containers** — Design, run, and harden clusters; package workloads with Helm; manage rollouts safely.
- **CI/CD & GitOps** — Enterprise-grade pipelines with Git as the single source of truth; automated, auditable deployments.
- **Infrastructure as Code** — Terraform-first infrastructure that's reviewable, repeatable, and version-controlled.
- **Automation** — Ansible (AWX / AAP) for configuration and operational workflows; scripting away the toil.
- **Cloud & SRE** — AWS and Azure environments built for reliability, observability, and sensible cost.
- **Enterprise Integration** — MuleSoft platform architecture and API-led connectivity across systems.
